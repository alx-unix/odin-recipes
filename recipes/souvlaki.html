Souvlaki Greek

Souvlaki is a Greek specialty made with tender cuts of meat. In this recipe, pork tenderloin is marinated in a lemony mixture of olive oil and oregano. Serve with rice pilaf and a Greek salad.

Ingredients

  • Pork meat
  • potato chips
  • olive oil
  • Vegetables
  • Skewers

Instructions

  1. Make marinade
  2. Thread the marinated ingredients onto skewers.
  3. Grill until the pork is no longer pink in the center.
